About me

I am Dhruma Jani, a new student at the Algonquin college this year. I was born on 23rd september 2004 in India. I am 18 years old and I am here to learn and develop skills in the creative field. Before transfering to IMD program, I was enrolled in Early Childhood Education program in Algonquin College itself. Being someone who has a keen interest in writing and reading, I have many quotes i believe in but there is this one quote I live by:

"Cause I can't forgive myself, if I am just a servant to my pain."

-Colby Brock

These lines are from a song named Skin by Colby Brock. These lines help me get out of bed everytime I am not feeling good about myself by reminding me that if I don't give my 100% and don't achieve the goals I have set for myself, I will not be able to forgive myself. Apart from being a writer I am also a student, a dancer and a MUA. I believe that creativity is what adds purpose to life no matter what type of creativity it is. That is all about me, I hope I didn't bore you.
